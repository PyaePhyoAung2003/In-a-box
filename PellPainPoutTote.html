Dumpling

  • This recipe is chinese dumpling with pork filling.
  • Contains: Eggs and flour in dumpling wrapper, soy, meat.
  • Allergens: Soy
  • Tools: Mixing bowls, frying pan with lid

Dumpling

  • dumpling wrappers
  • ground pork(1/2 lbs 225g)
  • red cabbage(2 cups/220g)
  • green onin(2 cups/200g),sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ginger, minced (4 tbsp)
  • soy sauce(2 tbsp)

Dumpling

  • salt(2 tsp)
  • peeper (1/2)
  • sesame oil(2 tbsp)
  • warm water (1 1/4 cups or 300 ml)

Step-by-step instructions

1)Prepare for fillings

Combine the cabbage, geen onions, gralic, ginger, soy saue, and sesame oil in a medium bowl and mix untial well-incorporated.

2)Mix with pork

Combine the ground pork with a teaspoon of salt, the pepper and the cabbage mixture and stir until well-incorporated.

Wrap the dumplings

To assemble the dumplings, add roughly 1 heaping tablespoon of filling to the center of a dumpling wrapper. With your finger, lightly coat half of the outside of the wrapper with water.Fold the moistened half of the wrapper over the filling and, using your fingers, pleat the edgs to seal. Repeat with the remaining fillings and wrappers.

4)Cook the dumplings

Heat the oil over medium high in a large skillet and add a few dumplings, cooking them in batches. Once the bottoms of the dumplings start to brown, add a splash of water and cover with a lid. Stem for about 5 minutes, or until the dimplins are cooked and the water has evaporated.Transfer the cooked dumplings to a paper towel-lined plate to remove any excess mosture or grease.

5)Making soy sause

In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and pepper flakes and stir to combine. Serve the dumplings immediately with the dipping sauce.
