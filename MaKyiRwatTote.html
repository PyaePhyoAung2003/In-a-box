MaKyiRwat Tote

Ingredients

  • 5 kyats for tea (sweet)
  • 2 eggplants (thinly sliced)
  • A small piece of cabbage (thinly sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons fried onion
  • 3 tablespoons peanuts
  • Sesame 2 tablespoons
  • 2 tablespoons fried beans
  • 1 tablespoon dried shrimp
  • Canned chicken powder 1 teaspoon
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon dried shrimp powder
  • 5 pods of green chillies (sliced)
  • 5 pods of green chillies
  • 4 tablespoons of oil
  • Half a teaspoon of sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 5 cloves of garli

MaKyiRwat Tote

How to cook

  • Add a tablespoon of oil to the first cup of tea, add chicken powder, 1 lemon, 5 cloves of garlic and 5 slices of green chillies.
  • Add onion, peanut, sesame, bean paste, dried shrimp powder, tomato and cabbage to the tea leaves.
  • Add 3 tablespoons of oil and knead by hand.
  • Finally, sprinkle the dried prawns on top and bite into the remaining five green chillies and garlic cloves.
