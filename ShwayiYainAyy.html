How to make a stone?

    1. Put water from a bottle of water in a large saucepan and bring to the boil.
    2. Then add all the sugar to the pan and let it melt.
    3. Then add a little water to the batter, add it to the pan and stir frequently until smooth.
    4. When it starts to thicken, add coconut milk and mix well.
    5. Then pour the juice into a bowl and let it cool.
    Note. : If you want to make two layers of gourd, pour half of the gourd juice into the pan before adding the coconut milk.
    Then add coconut milk, mix with the other half of the juice and pour on top.
