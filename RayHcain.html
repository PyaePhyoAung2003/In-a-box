RayHcain

    This recipe is for oily noodles.
    Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Chicken skin
  • garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • pepper
  • Wet or dry noodles
  • oil.

RayHcain

How to do it

  • Rinse the chicken skin and knead it Make a frying pan and add the chicken skins and roll them. Roll the chicken skins until they are crispy. The chicken fat will come out on its own.
  • Wash the chicken in water, season with salt, pepper, sauce, garlic and ginger and cook until tender. When tender, make small pieces of meat
  • Stir in the garlic. Add the puffed garlic in the oil and be careful not to burn it. The garlic is cooked.
  • If you use wet noodles, put the noodles in boiling water and recover
  • If you use dry noodles, boil the noodles and wash them

RayHcain

How to prepare

  • Add the noodles, chicken stock, chicken oil, garlic oil, chicken powder, soy sauce, chilli powder, onion and chicken powder and mix well. Then you have a delicious homemade chicken noodle
  • In addition to chicken, you can also use pork and turkey in the same way
  • You can buy chicken skins at a chicken shop, or you can make chicken skins with chicken skin.
  • If you do not have skin, you can eat it with garlic oil. Respectfully yours
