ToetHuu

(Claypot Chicken Rice)

    It is a very popular earthenware chicken rice in Singapore and Malaysia. Its specialty is the earthenware earthenware rice, which is a mixture of all kinds of chicken cooked in earthenware. It is popular for dinner in many shops.

ToetHuu

(Ingredients)

  • Rice - 3 cups rice
  • Chicken broth - 4 1/2 cups rice
  • Chicken - 300 grams
  • Ginger - Half a teaspoon
  • Soy sauce - a tablespoon
  • Black soy sauce - a tablespoon
  • Sesame oil - a tablespoon
  • Cornstarch / Glue - one tablespoon
  • Croissant oil - one tablespoon
  • Salt - a tablespoon
  • Chili - A little
  • Oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Fried onion - to sprinkle
  • Onion / Cilantro
  • Chili - to eat together

ToetHuu

(How to prepare)

    (1) Cut the chicken into small pieces and place in a bowl with salt, pepper, ginger, soy sauce, black pepper,sesame oil and sesame powder for 15 minutes.
    (2) Wash the rice and soak it for 15 minutes.
    (3) Put chicken stock in an earthenware pot or pot. Drain the rice and set aside.
    (4) When cooking in an earthenware pot, reduce the heat to a simmer. Spread the chicken on top of the steamer.
    (5) Then cover tightly and cook over low heat for 15 minutes to cook the chicken. Do not open the lid.
    (6) When the rice is cooked, you can mix the chicken with other dishes and serve the hot pottery rice with fried onion, coriander, onion and chili.
