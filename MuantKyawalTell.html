MuantKyawalTell

Ingredients

    1. 25 g of cornstarch (about 2 tablespoons)
    2. 250 ml of milk (1/4 of a 1 liter bottle)
    3. 50 ml of palm juice
    4. A little salt

MuantKyawalTell

How to do it

    Mix all the ingredients in a baking dish and cook over medium heat.
    Keep the pot on the stove. Do not light a fire.
    It takes about 3 minutes. Stir for another 3-4 minutes.
    The freezer is oiled. Pour into a freezer. Let cool
    If it freezes, it can be cut to any size. Sprinkle with unripe coconut and eat it cold in the freezer.
